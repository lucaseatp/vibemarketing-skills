## Core Principle

**Ecosystem first, aggressive budget second.**

Accounts with few validated ads saturate quickly when budget jumps — Meta lacks creative variety for automatic remarketing and CPA spikes.

Recommended order:

1. Validate offer + audience + creatives (1-1-3, 1-3-1 structures).
2. Isolate winners in **1-1-1** (multiple parallel campaigns).
3. Maintain **test pipeline** (continuous 1-1-3).
4. Then **increase budget** (daily scaling or cost cap — see `cost-cap-low-ticket.md`).

Operational reference: 10–15 profitable 1-1-1 campaigns before focusing only on raising budget on the same campaign.

## Phases: Ecosystem vs Budget

| Situation | Focus | Avoid |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1–3 creatives selling | Add **more campaigns** and **more creatives** | $100+/day on 1–2 ads |
| 8–15 stable campaigns | Budget scale on **best days** | Stop creative testing |
| High ROAS + low CPA **today** | Increase budget **that day** | Ignore and keep low fixed budget |
| Single creative in ecosystem | Urgent: validate **+3 to +5** creatives | Rely on one ad |

## Day Rule (ROAS + CPA)

Intraday: the **higher the ROAS** and **lower the CPA**, the more budget the campaign can take.

- Campaigns with ROAS 2.5–3.0 and CPA on target → candidates for **same-day** scale.
- Do not raise budget on **new** campaigns (< 48h) or **low volume** (< ~10 conversions in analyzed period).
- **Validated** campaigns (e.g. 16–28 sales in period) tolerate more aggressive budget (e.g. $35–100/day equivalent) than new ones at $8–12/day.

**Profit > theoretical ROAS:** ROAS 1.5–1.8 at high volume can profit more than ROAS 2.5 at $5/day. Decide on **absolute margin**, not multiple alone.

## Horizontal Stacking

Instead of scaling one campaign to the ceiling:

- Duplicate winning campaign → keep **audience** → change **creative** only (test).
- Add another **1-1-1** with validated creative (same audience or Advantage+ in parallel).
- Old campaigns with low ROAS but **cheap IC** can stay active — they add touchpoints and cheapen the ecosystem; don't pause after 5–7 days without sale if impressions/clicks stay cheap.

## Creative Testing Without Breaking the Winner

**Never** add a new creative inside a campaign that is already selling.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Duplicate campaign, keep audience, swap creative | Edit targeting/distribution on active campaign |
| Test **1-1-3** (max **3 ads** per ad set) | 4+ ads in same ad set (splinters budget) |
| Wait **≥ 48h** before killing new campaign | Pause after hours with no conversion |
| Test **same 3 creatives** across 3 audiences (1-3-1) | Change audience **and** creative at once without control |

After audience validated: **stop testing audience** — only **1-1-3** creative tests on winning audience.

**Advantage+:** with account data, duplicate validated creative to pure Advantage+ campaign (broad) — often outperforms detailed targeting alone.

## Ticket, Margin, Profit

- Ticket too low → "good" ROAS on paper, **zero profit**. Adjust pricing/plans before scaling.
- Order bumps / upsells raise AOV — use **RPU**, not base price alone.
- Ticket **< ~$20 USD equivalent**: prefer **static budget** + stacking + cost cap (see `cost-cap-low-ticket.md`), not aggressive daily scaling.

## Multiple Offers

Do not depend on a single offer — daily swings are normal.

- Stack ROAS across **multiple offers** (SaaS, ebook, low ticket, etc.) → stable total profit.
- Low ROAS on one offer can be ok if others compensate.
- Launch new offers **in parallel**; don't wait for current one to "stabilize forever."

## Engaged + Buyer Audiences

Refresh custom **engaged** and **buyer** audiences every **~30 days**.

Meta uses automatic remarketing — without new creatives in the ecosystem, remarketing saturates.

## Common Errors

- Single ad selling → inevitable saturation.
- Duplicate campaign **without** changing a variable (cannibalization).
- Test audience and creative in same matrix → can't tell what worked.
- Scale budget before **campaign/creative volume** exists.
- **Engagement → WhatsApp** campaign when goal is sale (no tracked conversion).
- Budget **< $3–4/ad set** in 1-1-3 with ~$6 ticket — won't deliver.
