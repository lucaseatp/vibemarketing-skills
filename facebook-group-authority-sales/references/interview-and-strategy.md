# Interview and Strategy

Use this reference before creating any content. The goal is to understand why the user has real relevance for a specific community.

## Essential Questions

- What is the niche, offer, service area, price range, and ideal customer?
- What is the operator's identity: owner, founder, professional, service provider, specialist, creator, consultant, salesperson, technician, etc.?
- What real ties do they have to the region or community?
- What real stories connect the operator to the neighborhood, city, group, or local problem?
- What real experiences can become content: a recommended place, event, local job, sponsorship, volunteer work, authorized before/after, or behind-the-scenes moment?
- Why should this specific group care about their presence?
- What proof exists: photos, testimonials, reviews, licenses, case studies, events, or partnerships?
- What contact path is allowed and natural: phone, website, or DM?
- Are there legal, licensing, insurance, privacy, customer image, or platform-rule restrictions?

## Required Synthesis

After the interview, summarize:

- The user's real authority.
- The user's real community tie.
- Usable stories.
- Usable proof.
- Limits on what cannot be said.
- Recommended tone for the operator.
- Allowed or preferred CTAs.

If real data is missing, use placeholders and ask for confirmation. Do not invent facts.
